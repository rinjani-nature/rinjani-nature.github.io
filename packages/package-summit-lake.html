Summit & Segara Anak Lake

3 Days / 2 Nights • The Complete Adventure

Overview

This is our most popular package for adventurous trekkers. You will experience everything Mount Rinjani has to offer: the challenge of the Summit, the tranquility of the Lake, and the natural Hot Springs.

  • Start Point: Sembalun Village (1,156m)
  • Finish Point: Senaru Village (601m)
  • Max Altitude: 3,726m (The Summit)
  • Difficulty Level: Challenging / Expert

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 Sembalun Village – Sembalun Crater Rim

Highlight: Savannah Trek & Sunset at Crater Rim.

  • 07:00 AM: Pick up from your hotel (Senggigi/Mataram area) and transfer to Sembalun.
  • 08:30 AM: Registration at Rinjani Information Center (RIC). Start trekking.
  • 12:00 PM: Lunch at Pos 2 (Tengengean).
  • 17:00 PM: Arrive at Sembalun Crater Rim (Camp Site). Enjoy the spectacular sunset over Segara Anak Lake.
  • Accommodation: Camping Tent.
  • Meals: Lunch, Dinner.

Day 2 Summit – Lake – Senaru Crater Rim

Highlight: Summit Sunrise, Swimming in the Lake & Hot Springs.

  • 02:00 AM: Wake up and light breakfast. Start the challenging climb to the summit.
  • 06:00 AM: Reach the summit of Mount Rinjani (3,726m). Sunrise view over Lombok, Bali, and Sumbawa.
  • 07:30 AM: Descent back to campsite for full breakfast.
  • 10:00 AM: Descent to Segara Anak Lake. Visit the natural hot springs (Aiq Kalak).
  • 14:00 PM: Ascent to Senaru Crater Rim (approx. 3 hours).
  • 17:30 PM: Camp at Senaru Crater Rim. Sunset view facing Mount Agung in Bali.
  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 3 Senaru Crater Rim – Senaru Village

Highlight: Tropical Rainforest & Wildlife.

  • 07:00 AM: Breakfast with a scenic view.
  • 08:00 AM: Descent through the tropical rainforest to Senaru Village. Better chance to see wildlife (Black Monkeys).
  • 13:00 PM: Arrive at Senaru Village. Lunch at our base.
  • 14:00 PM: Drop off to your next destination (Senggigi/Bangsal Harbor/Airport).
  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch.
