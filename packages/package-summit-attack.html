Summit Attack

2 Days / 1 Night • The Express Challenge

Package Overview

Short on time but want to stand on top of Indonesia?
Our "Summit Attack" package is designed for fit trekkers who want to conquer the summit of Mount Rinjani (3,726m) in just 2 days. This is a fast-paced, challenging trek starting and finishing in Sembalun Village.

We provide full service so you can focus on the climb. Our Professional Guides will ensure your safety, while our Porters carry all camping equipment and food. You just need to carry your small daypack.

  • Start Point: Sembalun Village (1,156m)
  • Finish Point: Sembalun Village
  • Max Altitude: 3,726m (The Summit)
  • Level: Challenging (Good fitness required)

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 Sembalun Village – Sembalun Crater Rim

Highlight: Trekking through the Savannah & Sunset above the clouds.

  • 07:00 AM: Pick up from your hotel (Mataram/Senggigi/Kuta Lombok) by private car. Transfer to Sembalun Village.
  • 08:30 AM: Registration at the Rinjani Information Center (RIC). Meet your Guide and Porters. Briefing about safety and trash management.
  • 09:00 AM: Start Trekking. The route begins with an open savannah (grassland). It can be hot, so sunblock is essential.
  • 12:00 PM: Lunch break at Pos 2 (Tengengean - 1,500m). Freshly cooked meal by our team.
  • 17:00 PM: Arrive at Sembalun Crater Rim (Pelawangan Sembalun - 2,639m).
  • 18:00 PM: Enjoy a magical sunset view overlooking the Segara Anak Lake. Dinner is served at the camp.
  • 19:00 PM: Early sleep to save energy for the summit attack.

Day 2 Summit Attack – Descent to Sembalun

Highlight: Golden Sunrise at the Summit (3,726m).

  • 02:00 AM: Wake up call. Light breakfast (Coffee/Tea, Biscuits, Toast).
  • 02:30 AM: Start the climb to the summit under the stars. The terrain involves loose volcanic sand and gravel ("2 steps up, 1 step down").
  • 06:00 AM: REACH THE SUMMIT (3,726m)! Witness the sunrise over Lombok, Bali (Mt. Agung), and Sumbawa (Mt. Tambora). A truly unforgettable moment.
  • 07:00 AM: Begin descent back to the campsite (approx. 1 hour).
  • 08:30 AM: Full breakfast at the campsite (Pancakes/Eggs/Fruit). Pack up.
  • 10:00 AM: Descent back to Sembalun Village via the same savannah route.
  • 15:00 PM: Arrive at Sembalun Village. Say goodbye to the porters.
  • 16:00 PM: Transfer back to your next destination or airport.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this trek difficult?

Yes, the summit push is physically demanding due to loose sand and altitude. We recommend this for trekkers with good fitness or previous hiking experience.

What should I carry in my daypack?

Just your personal items: Jacket, Headlamp, Sunblock, Camera, and Water bottle. The porters carry the rest.
