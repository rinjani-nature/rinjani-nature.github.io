4D3N via Sembalun

4 Days / 3 Nights • Summit, Lake Camping & Relaxed Pace

Package Overview

Want to enjoy Rinjani without rushing?
This 4 Days 3 Nights package is the perfect choice for photographers, families, or trekkers who want to truly soak in the atmosphere. Unlike the 3D2N package, you will spend a whole night camping right next to Segara Anak Lake.

You will have ample time to swim in the lake, relax in the natural hot springs, and enjoy the starry night sky reflected on the water.

  • Start Point: Sembalun Village (1,156m)
  • Finish Point: Senaru Village (601m)
  • Max Altitude: 3,726m (The Summit)
  • Pace: Relaxed & Enjoyable

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 Sembalun Village – Sembalun Crater Rim

Highlight: Savannah Trek & Sunset.

  • 07:00 AM: Pick up & transfer to Sembalun Village.
  • 08:30 AM: Registration at RIC. Start trekking through the open savannah.
  • 12:00 PM: Lunch at Pos 2 (Tengengean).
  • 17:00 PM: Arrive at Sembalun Crater Rim (Camp 1). Enjoy sunset.
  • Meals: Lunch, Dinner.

Day 2 Summit – Descent to Lake (Camping)

Highlight: Summit Sunrise, Hot Springs & Lake Camping.

  • 02:00 AM: Summit Attack (3,726m) for sunrise.
  • 07:00 AM: Return to camp for breakfast.
  • 10:00 AM: Descent to Segara Anak Lake (2,000m).
  • 13:00 PM: Arrive at the lake. Lunch.
  • Afternoon: Free time! Swim in the hot springs, go fishing, or relax.
  • Overnight: Camping at Segara Anak Lake (Camp 2). Bonfire & stargazing.
  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 3 Lake – Senaru Crater Rim

Highlight: Relaxed Morning & Crater Rim View.

  • Morning: Enjoy a slow morning at the lake. Breakfast.
  • 11:00 AM: Start hiking up to Senaru Crater Rim.
  • 15:00 PM: Arrive at Senaru Crater Rim (Camp 3).
  • 17:30 PM: The best sunset view spot facing Mount Agung (Bali).
  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 4 Senaru Rim – Senaru Village

Highlight: Tropical Rainforest & Wildlife.

  • 07:00 AM: Breakfast with a view.
  • 08:00 AM: Descent to Senaru Village through the lush rainforest.
  • 13:00 PM: Arrive at Senaru Village. Farewell lunch.
  • 14:00 PM: Drop off to your next destination.
  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch.
