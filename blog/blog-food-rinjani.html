Food on the Mountain: Dining at 2,000 Meters

By Rinjani Nature Team | Culture & Food

When you think of camping food, you might imagine instant noodles and cold sandwiches. Think again! On Mount Rinjani, our porters are not just carriers; they are mountain chefs. Prepare to be amazed by full-course meals cooked fresh every day, high above the clouds.

1. Freshly Cooked, Never Instant

We believe that good food is fuel for the summit. That’s why we bring fresh ingredients: eggs, vegetables, chicken, rice, and fresh fruits. Our porters carry kitchen equipment, including woks and gas stoves, to the campsite.

2. The Menu: What Will You Eat?

Here is a typical daily menu on our Rinjani Trekking Packages:

🥞 Breakfast

To start your day, we usually serve the trekkers' favorite: Banana Pancakes with honey or cheese. Accompanied by scrambled eggs, toast, and hot coffee/tea (Lombok coffee is famous!).

🍛 Lunch

During the break, enjoy Indonesian classics like Gado-Gado (vegetables with peanut sauce), Mie Goreng (fried noodles), or Vegetable Soup to warm you up.

🍗 Dinner

After a long day, feast on Nasi Goreng Special (Fried Rice with chicken/egg) or Chicken Curry with white rice and crackers (Kerupuk).

3. A Taste of Sasak Culture

We love introducing our guests to the local flavors of Lombok (Sasak cuisine). While we keep the spice level mild for international tourists, you will taste the authentic blend of shallots, garlic, and fresh herbs that make Indonesian food so delicious.

Pro Tip: Ask your guide for "Sambal" (chili paste) if you like it spicy!

4. Fresh Fruits & Snacks

Hydration and vitamins are key. During trekking breaks, our team will cut up fresh tropical fruits for you:

  • Pineapple (Nanas)
  • Watermelon (Semangka)
  • Oranges (Jeruk)

We also provide chocolate biscuits and plenty of mineral water.

5. Dietary Requirements (Vegetarian/Vegan)

Do you have allergies? Are you Vegetarian, Vegan, or Gluten-Free?

No problem! Just let us know when you book. Since we cook everything from scratch, we can easily adjust the ingredients. Tempeh and Tofu are excellent protein sources available on our vegetarian menu.
