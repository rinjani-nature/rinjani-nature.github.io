Plan Your Trip to Lombok

The Ultimate Guide for Dutch Travelers 2026

Why Choose Lombok?

The perfect mix of adventure and relaxation.

Less Crowded than Bali

Enjoy authentic Indonesia. Lombok offers pristine beaches, traditional Sasak villages, and quieter roads compared to its neighbor, making it perfect for a relaxed holiday.

World-Class Trekking

Home to Mount Rinjani (3,726m), one of the most spectacular volcano treks in Asia. A bucket-list experience for many Dutch outdoor enthusiasts.

The Gili Islands

Just a short boat ride away. No cars, crystal clear water, and turtles. The perfect place to recover after your Rinjani climb.

Logistics: Getting Here from NL

✈️ Flights from Amsterdam (AMS)

There are no direct flights to Lombok (LOP) from Europe. The best routes for Dutch travelers are:

  • Via Jakarta (CGK): Fly KLM or Garuda Indonesia to Jakarta, then take a domestic flight (2 hours) to Lombok.
  • Via Bali (DPS): Fly to Bali, then take a 30-minute flight or a fast boat (Padang Bai to Bangsal) to Lombok.
  • Via Singapore (SIN): Singapore Airlines offers connections to Lombok.

🛂 Visa Requirements

Dutch citizens (and most EU nationals) can obtain a Visa on Arrival (VoA) at Lombok Airport. Cost is approx. €30-35 (IDR 500,000), valid for 30 days and extendable once.

☀️ Best Time to Visit

April – November (Dry Season): The best time for trekking Rinjani. July and August correspond with Dutch summer holidays, so booking in advance is essential.

🔌 Travel Essentials

Indonesia uses 2-pin sockets (Type C/F), same as the Netherlands. Bring Reef-safe sunscreen and sturdy hiking boots if you plan to trek.
